Vercel Sandbox now supports persistent, attachable storage called Drives in private beta, allowing users to mount storage across multiple sandboxes with independent lifecycles. Drives can be mounted in read-write mode and are particularly useful for maintaining agent workspaces and retaining repositories and dependencies between disposable sandbox instances.
