Node.js and Python runtimes now extend execution to 30 minutes (from 800 seconds) on Pro and Enterprise plans. Fluid Compute charges active CPU time only and pauses billing during I/O, supporting LLM reasoning, streaming responses, document processing, OCR, scraping, and complex workflows.
