A public beta release enabling scoped token requests at runtime, with connectors for Slack, GitHub, Linear, Discord, Notion, Salesforce, Figma, Snowflake, plus OAuth and API key support. Pricing is 5K tokens monthly free on Hobby and $3 per 10K on higher plans.
